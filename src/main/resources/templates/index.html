It's Nice To Meet You
We are a group of Christians based in Bucharest, Romania, who assist Ukrainians in Romania, Moldova, and those transiting to other countries, in whatever ways they need.

Thank you for your support!

Financial

Thanks to the monetary donations, our volunteers are able to provide assistance as soon as needed.

Where you can donate:
  • Global Outreach Missionary Emergency Fund
  • Moldova's Paypal account
How the donations are being used:
  • Fuel for transportation of supplies.
  • Buying supplies for refugees, like food, toiletries, clothing, over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, small toys, outdoor gear, etc.
  • Supplies for the buildings that host refugees: cleaning, heating, food, etc
  • Financial support for refugees going to other countries.
  • If you want to know more about our work, check Naty Tully's facebook profile.

Volunteer

Our volunteers help with various work, including sorting donations, packing "grab bags" with essential supplies to give to our guests from Ukraine, shopping, supply runs to the border and ministries in Moldova, transporting our guests, etc.

We love to utilize each volunteer's strengths and abilities, but we may also call on you to stretch beyond your comfort areas to help our guests and those crossing the border. These things can include calling or messaging churches in other countries to help our guests as they travel to new locations, shopping for large quantities of items, and researching any number of things if needed.
